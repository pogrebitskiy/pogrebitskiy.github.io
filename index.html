Projects

Financial Data and Stock Price Correlation

Nov 2021 - Dec 2021

This project analyzed the correlation between financial data and stock prices of Apple and Google from 2000 to 2021. Over 600 variables were considered, and the analysis involved calculating ratios and graphing them against stock prices. The results showed unpredictable relationships between certain metrics and stock prices. Clear graphs with linear regression were presented. Future steps include expanding the analysis to other companies and considering recent years of data.
